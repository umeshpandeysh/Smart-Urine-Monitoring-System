# Urinalysis Reference Materials & Standard Links

This file lists clinical resources, color charts, and sensor datasheets to support research and development of the **Smart Urine Monitoring System**.

---

## 1. Clinical Standards & Reagent Parameters

* **National Institutes of Health (NIH) Urinalysis Guideline**:
  * [MedlinePlus - Urinalysis Standard Values](https://medlineplus.gov/ency/article/003579.htm)
  * Details healthy range values for pH (4.6 to 8.0), specific gravity (1.002 to 1.030), and explains normal versus abnormal trace parameters.
* **CDC Urinary Tract Infection (UTI) Detection Indicators**:
  * [CDC - UTI Urinalysis Standard](https://www.cdc.gov/antibiotic-use/community/pdfs/FINAL-UTI_Diagnosis_Flyer.pdf)
  * Discusses clinical sensitivity of Nitrites and Leukocyte Esterase for bacterial detection.

---

## 2. Sensor Datasheets & Hardware Docs

* **TCS34725 Color Light-to-Digital Converter**:
  * [AMS TCS34725 Datasheet](https://ams.com/documents/20143/36005/TCS34725_DS000307_2-00.pdf)
  * Technical specifications on spectral responsivity, registers, integration times, and I2C commands.
* **DS18B20 Programmable Resolution 1-Wire Digital Thermometer**:
  * [Maxim Integrated DS18B20 Datasheet](https://datasheets.maximintegrated.com/en/ds/DS18B20.pdf)
  * Explains 1-wire communication protocol, parasitically powered modes, and memory map structure.
* **MQ-2 Gas Sensor Details**:
  * [SparkFun MQ-2 Guide](https://www.sparkfun.com/datasheets/Sensors/Biomed/MQ-2.pdf)
  * Characteristic curves of resistance ratio ($Rs/Ro$) for various combustible gases, VOCs, and alcohol fumes.
